RESULTS

Glazing the pot with latex paint will result in a significant difference compared to commercial pots in terms of deformities and its physical, mechanical, and environmental properties when exposed to extremely high temperatures, such as the heat inside the oven. However, exposing the pots to lower temperatures, such as those inside the freezer or outdoors, will not create a significant difference between the two groups of pots. The physical properties of the latex-painted pots remain the same, while their mechanical properties, such as hardness and toughness, improve. The brittleness of the latex-painted pots is slightly reduced. The environmental properties of the latex-painted pots remain unchanged, except for temperature, where the higher the temperature, the more significant the difference between the two groups.
