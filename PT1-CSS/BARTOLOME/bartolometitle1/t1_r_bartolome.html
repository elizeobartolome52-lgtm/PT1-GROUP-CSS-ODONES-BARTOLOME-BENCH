RESULTS

The respondents seem to like watercolor that have too much pigment, the color is
vibrant and intense, and the texture is good but the respondents sees a few
problems like the organic watercolor is watery and it smells terrible which are cause
by the changing of temperature of water and the amount of petals. At the same
time, the respondents prefer organic watercolor because it is environmental friendly
and does not contain any bad chemicals.
